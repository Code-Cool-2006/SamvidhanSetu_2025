The Immigration and Foreigners Bill, 2025

Status:Passed

Sponsor:Ministry of Home Affairs

Summary:The Immigration and Foreigners Bill, 2025 seeks to modernize India's immigration framework by streamlining visa categories, enhancing border security, and improving regulations for foreign nationals. It replaces outdated laws like the Foreigners Act, 1946, and introduces stricter provisions for illegal immigration. The bill also establishes a centralized National Immigration Authority to oversee all immigration-related matters, ensuring efficiency and security.
